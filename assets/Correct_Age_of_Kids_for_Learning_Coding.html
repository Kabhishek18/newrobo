Correct Age of Kids for Learning Coding

What is the correct age of kids for learning coding in the 21st century?

October 03, 2014
  • Anna Brown
  • Events, Fun
  • Events, Fun

Parents are always in a dilemma of what to invest in for their kids. They want to choose something that could excite the kids and at the same time, it helps them to learn and grow.To solve this dilemma, you can offer the best to your child by enrolling them in coding courses.

Coding is essential in this 21st century digital era and will be the future language. Coding is the process of giving instructions to the computer that translates it into a language that the computer understands. Basically, the role of a coder is to write instructions using a programming language. The basis of any software, apps and websites is coding.


If individual masters the programming early in life, then there the chances of a successful career are high. Programmers are desired in almost all industries.As per the recent statistics, more than 65% of jobs in the coming years would require good knowledge of programming skills. Their average salary will be higher as compared to other industries. A person can get a dream package in the starting years.


Earlier lot of people were not aware of the significance of coding, but now it is a growing trend to learn to code. The demand for learning to code is on a rise among children.

What does coding do?

Coding expands the horizon of kids and makes them do innumerable things. As we all know, it is a digital age and everything is computerized nowadays. Through coding, children can explore their creative side. As learning coding may be complex, so there are so many new ways of teaching coding that the kids will learn coding by playing games, creating animation and characters. Now it has become fun and engaging.


Children are exposed to the practical environment through live projects.For better understanding, whatever they learn, they execute in the projects.Coding can be learned as early as 6 years. For the young learners, a block-based visual programming language is used to create interactive stories, music, animations and games.

What are the numerous apps or games that make coding engaging and fun?

There are multiple possibilities in coding:

  • Bring ideas to reality -Make animating characters

  • Draw graphics.

  • Make puzzles

  • Learning mathematical expressions

  • Concepts of science

  • Developing a racing game

  • Creation of calculation app

  • Memorization games

  • Color mixing lamp

  • Keyboard piano

  • Memorization games

  • Create digital assistants

Scope of Artificial Intelligence in India

The world has become quite competitive and the parents must plan to provide education that could be beneficial and entertaining for the kids. The government has also understood the importance of coding and trying to introduce it in the initial years. According to the new education policy laid down by the government, coding will be considered as a mandatory course from Class 6 onwards.
