What do you learn inAnimation and Game Design course?

A block-based visual programming language that allows children to experience a block-like interface to craft your own interactive stories, games, music and animations.Through this course, we aim to provide basic computer skills through Drag and Drop programming.

Prerequisites for undertaking the course

A laptop with an internet connection and camera

Who can enroll?

We have designed this course for the children belonging to the age bracket of 6-12 years.

Key benefits of the course

This is a lighthearted side of programming that create bothfun and functional programs. When your child learns to code, they also learn some significant strategies for problem-solving, project designing, and a way of communicating ideas.


Just wonder what all your child can do with this program

  • Mathematical Expressions
  • Concepts of Science
  • Loop Sequencing
  • Conditional Statements

As we want them to have an enjoyable learning experience, so coming to the fun part, they will be enthralled with the way they can bring their stories and characters to life. They can design stories with numerous scenes, animations, innumerable characters and dialogues.All this is possible with the help of code that they will be writing to create their own version of stories.

A wide array of animation tools is used to create such beautiful characters and stories. Your kids can learn the basics as a hobby and may opt to become a professional animator later. It encourages your kid to think creatively and reason systematically.

Post completion of the course, your child will be good at ideating and designing.We are offering cost-effective courses so you can browse through our website for the ideal course for your child.
