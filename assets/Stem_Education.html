Stem Education

How STEM Education is fruitful for students?

October 03, 2014
  • Anna Brown
  • Events, Fun
  • Events, Fun

Children are always intrigued and they have endless questions in mind about each and everything like how objects float or how does a train run. Their minds are not confined to a few things. At a young age, they are always open-minded. They want to know about their surroundings and how things work near them.

Sometimes even after so much interest, they get bored easily with the classes. They have a short attention span and gets diverted quickly. They lose interest when conventional teaching is on. If we make STEM learning interesting by showing examples that they could relate to, then it is easier for them to grasp the knowledge and they will learn at ease with the fun activities. They will always be looking forward to learning more if they have a creative learning environment.

What is STEM?

A curriculum formed by grouping four academic disciplines Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics is what is popularly known as STEM.If STEM education is imparted in a fun way in the initial years, then it leads to the long-term interest in the four disciplines.

Advantages of STEM Education

STEM learning is quite significant and having a command over these four subjects opens the door to numerous opportunities in the future. It is vital for a successful career and one can surely apply for the in-demand and highest paid jobs in the later stage of life.

The skills of children can be developed by solving practical problems. STEM Education is fruitful for students as it helps in critical thinking, problem-solving skills, collaboration skills like open-mindedness, adaptability and interpersonal skills and persistence.

How does Coding helps in STEM Education?

In a creative environment, learning can happen in the best way. It helps the children to have a positive frame of mind and makes them open to learning the concept. The best way to inculcate STEM education will be to make them understand through fun sessions of coding.

Whatever they read and learn in textbooks, they get an opportunity to implement with hands-on experience and understand the why and how of a concept. An effective way to teach them will be to promote an environment through communication with others.

In this digital era, everything is technology-oriented. We have seen an inclination towards technology and gadgets for learning.

Through STEM, they can understand how things work in the real world.

The most engrossing STEM projects that the kidscan be taught through coding are:
  • Make your Piano- A keyboard piano can be created with a preset song using Arduino with Tinker Cad

  • Greeting Card-Through block coding, create a musical greeting card

  • Automatic Street Light- Create a working street light with the help of Arduino with Tinker Cad

  • Animation of Family NameCreate colorful animating family names using Python coding and programming

  • Game Design- A gaming app to be created throughonlineApplication Development software
