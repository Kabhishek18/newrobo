What is App Development?

An online application that allows the students to create an application software for two operating systems- Android and iOS. The applications are created by dragging and dropping visual objects that can run on Android devices.

Prerequisites for undertaking the course

A laptop with an internet connection and camera

Who can enroll?

This course is designed for children above 12 years of age.

Key benefits of the course

App Development uses block-based programming. It allows the children to build their own apps.It will help the children to gain a spherical perspective on the working of apps.


The topics we will cover are:

  • Memorization games to teach lists and loops
  • Creation of a calculation app
  • Mobile gamedevelopment basics
  • Programming fundamentals
  • Designing a user interface for Android

Through this course, our aim is that students should swiftly navigate through the ever-evolving technology.

As there is a rise in the demand for apps at present and the same will increase in the future, knowledge and getting trained in this will help the future generationto contribute to technology and engage with the world through technology.
